Bio

Willie is a common bottlenose dolphin, and is the present ruler of Dolphin World, alongside his older sisters Sarah and Deborah, younger sister Dorothy, and younger brother Samsam. His royal title is "Third King Willie the Third", as he's both the third oldest of his siblings, as well as the third king named Willie in Dolphin World's history.

Willie's birthday is August 9th, 2006.

Abilities

Willie is blessed with the powers of fire, allowing him the use of all sorts of skills to manipulate heat. His favorite move is simply breathing fire through his mouth. It's a versatile attack against any lurking enemies!

It is rumored that Willie's fire powers were granted to him by the first mortal grandmother of all dolphin world, Debbie.

Willie is often seen wearing a loose-fitting sewn bag as clothing. This bag is inhabited by a Willdoll spirit, though Willie just calls it "Bag". Bag is one of Willie's closest confidants, and serves as a defensive layer of armor for him.
