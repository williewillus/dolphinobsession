Bio

Dumpling Junior goes by Jun, and is Dumple's third child and only son. Coincidentally, he and his three sisters were all born on the same day.

Abilities

Jun is proficient at electricity powers, and is also a skilled swimmer, owing to his naturally sleek and handsome fur.

Like his father, Jun is an excellent cook.

Likes

  • Veggie dumples: Jun loves vegeterian dumplings, much to the disappointment of his father, who prefers meat dumplings instead.
  • His sisters

Dislikes

  • His dad bothering him about not liking meat dumplings.
